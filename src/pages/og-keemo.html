OG Keemo

Artist

Mannheim’s OG Keemo is widely regarded as one of the most gifted lyricists in German rap, crafting cinematic, deeply narrative verses that read like short films from the margins. JOKER translated that intensity into still frames — heavy, deliberate, and impossible to look away from.
