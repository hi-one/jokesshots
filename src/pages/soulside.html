Soulside

Event

Soulside is an ongoing artist collective and events project rooted in Düsseldorf. What started in the basement of the burger joint 5Pointz developed into one of the most vibrant soul and hip-hop artist communities and newcomer stages, with over two years of live events and counting. Soulside collaborators include the notorious Düsseldorf ‘Büdchentag’, New Fall Festival, and MTV 50 Years of Hip-Hop. JOKER has been there from the start, documenting the collective’s evolution from basement sessions to city-wide stages.
