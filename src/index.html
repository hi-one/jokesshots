About

Joker

A gentle giant off-camera — an absolute menace behind it. What sets Joker apart is how much emotion he compresses into a single frame. Pride, anger, joy, success, love — sometimes all at once. He can be within breathing range of an Ice Cube or a Burna Boy and still walk away with a candid that feels more honest than a portrait session ever could. From Summerjam’s blinding stage lights to the sweat-soaked basements of Düsseldorf’s Soulside, his eye for drama and detail stays the same.

Then there’s the grading. Joker’s signature color work takes raw frames and turns them into something that hits different — the cherry on top of an already loaded image. It’s the reason his shots don’t just document a moment, they make you feel like you were standing right there.
